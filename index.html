How to Join the Fight Organization

There are certain criteria to become a fighter and participate in the organization. Fighters can initially apply to our federation by submitting a medical report, passing a proficiency test, and paying $15,000. All fighters will receive a fixed $20,000 per fight even if they lose. Championship matches are held on the 5th of every month, and those who hold a title will receive an extra $20,000 and double pay for all matches. Contracts belong to the VFO company, which also reserves the right to terminate them.

About ticket prize, How to buy a ticket

Ticket sales belong to the VFO company, and each ticket is priced at $2,000. You must send the 'Bank ID' and a screenshot of the payment to the Discord official 'axellsus_7'. No one under the age of 18 is allowed to attend the event. After purchasing a ticket, you will not have any right to a refund."
